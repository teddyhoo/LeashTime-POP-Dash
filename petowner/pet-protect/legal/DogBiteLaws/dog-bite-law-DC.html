LEGAL PRINICIPLE Contributory negligence: if the victim contributes even 1% to the dog bite incident, then the dog bite law can completely obliterate the legal claims of the victim. This is also known as the "one bite rule".
THE POOP The District of Columbia is a "contributory negligence" jurisdiction regarding dog bite law. This is extremely dog owner friendly, because the plaintiff cannot even conribute 1% to the dog bite incident. This means the plaintiff nullifies all possible damage recovery if they are found to have contributed to the dog bite incident, no matter how egregiously negligent the dog owner had been.
