BASIC INFO

FOOD ITEM IMAGE METHOD OF ACTION SYMPTOM
Chocolate Theobromine is the toxic ingredient, particulary in dark chocolate and baking chocolate. Signs of chocolate toxicity occur hours after ingestion and include: hyperexcitability, vomiting, frequent urination, diarrhea, rapid breathing, weakness, seizures, coma. Death is rare but occurs by cardiac arrest.
Raisins and Grapes Fatal kidney failure. Most dogs will vomit some within hours of eating. Stop eating, diarrhea, become quiet with signs of abdominal pain. Eventually blood calcium levels go up and kidney failure follows.
Macadamia Nuts 7/18/16 Mild to severe weakness in the rear legs. Dogs seem to recover with time but activated charcoal treatment accelerates.
Onions and Garlic Hemolytic anemia, usually not causing acute toxicity. Induce vomiting and administer activated charcoal. Induce vomiting
Raw bread dough 7/18/16 Induce vomiting
Xylitol (artificial sweetner) Artificial sweetener foudn in gums, toothpaste and mints. Can cause low blood sugar and liver toxicity. Induce vomiting
Avocado Contains persin, which is in the leaves, seed, and bark. Induce vomiting
Caffeine In coffee, coffee beans, tea, grounds and energy drinks. Induce vomiting
